OUR SERVICES

Blood grouping and typing

A blood type is a classification of blood, based on the presence and absence of antibodies antibody an organism and inherited antigenic substances on the surface of red blood cells.

Blood cross matching

Crossmatching is a way for your healthcare provider to test your blood against a donor's blood to make sure they are fully compatible. Crossmatching takes 45 minutes to an hour.

Antibody titre

An antibody titer is a measurement of how much antibody an organism has produced that recognizes a particular epitope, expressed as the inverse of the greatest dilution that still gives a positive result.
